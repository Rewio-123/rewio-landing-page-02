OUR STORY

ORIGIN

Every gamer aspires to break into esports, but only a select few manage to do so. Despite the abundance of talented players, there are limited avenues to secure a spot in esports organizations. We set out to create opportunities for those gamers who may have been overlooked, aiming to kindle the passion in every player's journey towards esports.

THE JOD
